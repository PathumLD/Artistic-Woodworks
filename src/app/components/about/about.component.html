Company History

Founded in 2010, PathumLD began with a simple vision: to elevate wood as an artistic medium and create pieces that blend form and function in harmonious ways.

Our journey started in a small workshop with just three artists passionate about woodwork. Today, we've grown into a renowned studio that has completed over 200 installations across the country.

We believe in the transformative power of wood art to change spaces and create emotional connections between people and their environments.

Our Team

Behind every PathumLD creation is a team of passionate artisans, engineers, and visionaries who bring expertise from diverse backgrounds.

Our team is led by master sculptor Raymond Hayes, whose work has been exhibited in galleries worldwide, alongside our head of design Lisa Chen and master woodworker James Rodriguez.

We combine traditional woodworking techniques with cutting-edge technology to push the boundaries of what's possible with wood as an artistic medium.
